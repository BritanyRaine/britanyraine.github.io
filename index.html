Shadows Above Sanction

The Convergence did not arrive with the subtlety of a storm on the horizon or the soft tremors of an earthquake. Thirteen years ago, it began as a trickle—maddened beasts clawing their way from the abyss into the mortal plane. Over two years, it escalated into a cosmic collision as Heaven, Hell, and dimensions unknown merged with Earth, unleashing a cataclysm that reshaped reality.

Cities burned, oceans boiled, and skies fractured, leaving behind a new world both wondrous and terrifying. From this chaos, the city of San Jose was reborn as Sanction—a shattered metropolis where the remnants of humanity coexist uneasily with Hellkin and otherworldly beings. Here, morality is a luxury, survival is a gamble, and alliances shift like shadows.

In this crucible of blood and fire, lives intertwine in unexpected ways. Ash Britain, a mercenary with a haunted past, and Layla, a fierce Hellhound navigating her own fractured loyalties, find themselves bound together in a city where every choice carries unseen consequences. As conspiracies unravel, loyalties are tested, and the balance between realms teeters on the edge, Ash and Layla must confront not only the horrors of their world but the secrets of their own hearts.

Shadows Above Sanction is a dark fantasy epic where survival means sacrifice, and the line between light and darkness is no longer clear.
